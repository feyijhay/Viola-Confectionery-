Face Behind the Brand

Meet Elegbede Victoria Abosede

I am the founder and owner of Violabakes_ng, a confectionery brand built on passion, precision, and purpose. Before the flour and frosting, I walked the halls of banking, and that discipline shaped the way I run every aspect of this business.

Baking was never just a skill for me. It was a language. A way of showing up for people in their most meaningful moments. I kept refining that language, investing in my craft until every creation spoke for itself.

Beyond the kitchen, I work with credible brands as an influencer and content creator. I do not just promote products. I tell stories that connect, build trust, and turn audiences into loyal communities. If your brand deserves to be seen, I will make sure the right people see it.

I am an introvert with a big heart, a wife supported by a man who believes in every dream I carry, and a woman of faith who pours that faith into everything she creates. Welcome to my world.
